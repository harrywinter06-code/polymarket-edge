Limitations

Every headline number above is gross of execution cost on the Hyperliquid side and top-of-book on the Polymarket side. The full self-audit lives in REDTEAM.md. Sample size on the Hyperliquid backtest is 365 days (1,093 rebalances at the 8h cadence, 12 majors). Sharpe is the carry-only upper bound — net of 5 bp/leg spread the 8h cadence collapses to −207%; deployable region is the ≥ 2-weekly slice of the cadence frontier.
