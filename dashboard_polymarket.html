Limitations

Polymarket fees are per-category (Sports 0.75%, Politics 1.0%, etc.); the depth pass is mandatory before sizing because top-of-book reads lie on thin-side legs. The trap classifier is at n=30 today: scaffolding-grade, growing forward via the daily scan cron.
